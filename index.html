BLACKRAT only. You never get transferred to whoever is building — one contact, one person managing your project from briefing through delivery.

Yes, if the scope is clear. We care about the outcome, not the price. A scoped small project beats a vague large one every time.

50% upfront, 50% on delivery. No exceptions. This protects both sides and maintains focus on the work.
Do you offer revisions?+
Yes, within the agreed scope. If scope changes, that's a new conversation. Clear scope prevents revision creep from the start.

Usually within a few days depending on project and workload. First briefing happens fast. Timeline depends on complexity and budget.
